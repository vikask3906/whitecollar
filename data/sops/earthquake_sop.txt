NATIONAL DISASTER MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY (NDMA)
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE — EARTHQUAKE EMERGENCY
=====================================================

1. IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (0-15 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────
1.1. NDMA activates National EOC upon NCS/USGS alert (M ≥ 5.0).
1.2. Issue nationwide "Drop, Cover, Hold On" advisory.
1.3. All first responders report to nearest staging area.
1.4. Search and rescue teams deploy within 30 minutes of event.
1.5. Hospitals activate Mass Casualty Protocol (triage at entrance).
1.6. Gas pipelines and high-voltage power shut off in affected area.
1.7. Aftershock monitoring — warn public of secondary collapse risk.

2. SEARCH & RESCUE PRIORITIES
──────────────────────────────
2.1. Priority 1: Collapsed multi-story residential buildings.
2.2. Priority 2: Schools, hospitals, crowded commercial buildings.
2.3. Priority 3: Infrastructure — bridges, flyovers, dams.
2.4. Use heavy equipment ONLY after light rescue phase (0-72h).
2.5. Mark searched buildings with INSARAG marking system.

3. RESOURCE DEPLOYMENT
──────────────────────
3.1. 1 NDRF battalion per district in affected zone.
3.2. Urban SAR teams with dogs, cameras, and listening devices.
3.3. Field hospitals: 1 per 10,000 population in impact zone.
3.4. Army engineering units for road clearance.
3.5. IAF airlift for areas with road access disrupted.

4. COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL
──────────────────────────
4.1. All communication on NDRF Emergency Channel.
4.2. Satellite phones deployed to forward operating bases.
4.3. Citizen helpline: 1078 (NDMA Disaster Helpline).
4.4. Social media monitoring cell activated for SOS signals.
4.5. Status updates to PMO every 60 minutes.

5. SHELTER & RELIEF
────────────────────
5.1. Tent camps set up in open grounds — avoid buildings with cracks.
5.2. Distribution: 10 kg food grain, 10L water per family per day.
5.3. Blankets, medical kits, sanitation material within first 12 hours.
5.4. Psychological first aid teams deployed to all relief camps.

6. POST-EARTHQUAKE
───────────────────
6.1. Rapid Visual Screening of all structures in impact zone.
6.2. Red-tag (unsafe), Yellow-tag (restricted), Green-tag (safe).
6.3. Demolition of Red-tagged structures within 30 days.
6.4. Reconstruction guidelines per IS 1893 (seismic code).
6.5. Lessons learned report to NDMA within 90 days.
